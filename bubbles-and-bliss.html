FAQ

Simple answers for shoppers and gifting customers.

Do you take bulk or return-gift orders?

Yes. The website is structured around inquiry-led gifting, especially for return gifts, small events, and custom-packed orders.

Can I request custom colors?

Yes. Hanging soaps are showcased as available in different colors, so customization can be highlighted as a key selling point.

Are prices fixed on every product?

Not always. Some cards intentionally use “DM for price” or “custom quote” because handcrafted catalogs often change by quantity, packing style, or variant.

How do I place an order?

You can direct visitors to Instagram DM or WhatsApp. Both call-to-action patterns are already built into the site design.

Can the catalog be updated later?

Yes. Every product card in this build is easy to edit, so you can swap in exact product images, revised names, prices, and new seasonal items.

Can I use my own custom domain on Vercel?

Yes. Once the site is deployed, you can connect your own domain from the Vercel project settings.
