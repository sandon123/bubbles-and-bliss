Common questions.

Are your soaps suitable for kids?

Yes, we design gentle options and kids toy soaps, but if your child has very sensitive skin, please share details so we can guide appropriately.

Do you take return-gift and bulk orders?

Absolutely. We regularly prepare return gifts and bulk orders. Share your quantity, date, and theme on WhatsApp to get started.

Can I customise colours, shapes, or tags?

Yes, we love custom orders. We can suggest combinations based on your theme and prepare matching tags or simple packaging.

How much time do you need to prepare an order?

For small orders, 3–5 days is usually enough. Bulk and return-gift orders are best booked at least 1–2 weeks in advance.
